UNRAVEL ME

The ethereal “Unravel Me” reveals Claudio's commitment issues and
underlying insecurities throughout a relationship. The track unearths the Virgo
in Claudio as she bears her gentle indecisiveness and timidness in pursuing
connections. The track was accompanied by a sensual music video that
features the vulnerable songstress covered in pastel flowers and hypnotic
butterflies.
